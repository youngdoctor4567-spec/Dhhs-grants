DHHS Grants Program

Financial Support for All Walks of Life

The Department of Health and Human Services offers grants to help people from all backgrounds - whether you're working, a student, retired, young or old, deaf or hearing, widowed, or living with a disability. Our financial support programs make life easier for those in need.

Who We Help

Working Professionals

Financial assistance for those facing unexpected hardships while employed.

Students

Education grants and living expense support for students in need.

Seniors & Retirees

Special programs to help with medical expenses and quality of life improvements.

People with Disabilities

Accessibility grants and support services tailored to individual needs.
